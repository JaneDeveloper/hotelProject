History of Our Hotel

Our hotel was opened in 2005 by a group of young veterinarians who are cat lovers. Once they won 1 billion lottery and invested this money in building this amazing hotel and opened a huge animal shelter, which is located near the hotel. If you want to adopt another fluffy friend, you can do this at our hotel. All cats are vaccinated against rabies every year. These veterinarians keep working here and would be happy to answer all your questions about everything that concern you. Nowadays our hotel became a place of interest, a great number of people come here to play with cats who wait for their owners.

Although we are happy to see people who love spending time with our little friends, they don't have access to our guests. It means that strangers are not allowed to interact with your pets except our staff. You should not be worried about your pets because the territory is fenced and protected. We also have security cameras inside and outside the hotel. We have an amazing backyard for our beloved little predators if your pet is an outdoor cat and loves adventures. Moreover, in our hotel cats do not spend their time in uncomfortable cages. They can run wherever they want. We have a lot of toys and different catâ€™s houses where they can hide and have a rest.

Care about every guest

Every guest is important for use. If your cat is on a special diet, has issues with using a litter box, prefers a particular brand of litter, has paralysed forelimbs or hindlimbs, suffers obesity, an epileptic seizur, we are ready to help and make your pet's staying at our hotel as comfortable as possible. If your cats are on medication or they need to be injected everyday, you should not be worried about it, our veterinarians do everything that your cats need. If your pet has a difficult personality, for example the cat is not friendly or even agressive, you should not be worried about this either, we have specialists who find a common language with combative cats.

If your cat is pregnant or has recently become a mother, we will care after her and her kittens. We have a separate rooms for mothers and their babies, it protects babies from adult cats. If you have more than one cat, we will find space for everyone. Unfortunately, we don't deal with dogs, although we love them too. We can't allow your dog to stay at our hottel even if it has the same size that a cat has. Dogs are forbidden inside and outside our hotel.
