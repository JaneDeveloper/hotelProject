Our Services

Vaccination

For extra money we vaccinate your fluffy friend against rabies. We have specialists who examine your pet and give reccomendations on their weight and diet. Moreover, they examine your pet for fleas.

Walking

We walk with your pet. We use cat strollers, so you should not be worried that our friend will run away. If your friend stays at the hotel in winter, we provide warm coat, hat and socks and boots.

Cats' Menu

We have a menu for our guests. If your pet has a special diet or has preferences, you can examine the menu and let our staff know which your pet would like to eat during their staying at the hotel.

Cutting Nails and Hair

For extra money we can wash and cut your pet's hair if you are interested in this service. Moreover, we cut your pet's nails. We use only shampoos, which don't irritate their skin.

Cats' Toys

If your pet likes toys and other accessories in our hotel, you will be able to buy them at the cats' store, which is located inside the hotel. The prices there are lower than in other pets' shops.

Birthday Party

We organise birthday parties for our guests. If your friend has a birthday while they are staying at our hotel, we will be happy to make this day memorable for your pet.
